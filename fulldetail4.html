Nyarutarama, Kigali City.

DESCRIPTION:

The one-bedroom apartments at Nyarutarama are ideally designed for guests checking in as individuals or lovers. The
spacious interior may also cater to a couple with not more than one infant.

The in-house facilities, such as the private balcony and well groomed lawn, shared-gym, and spacious front yard, as
well as the neighbourhood amenities such as the tennis court, golf course, bars and restaurants will ensure most
things you'll need is at your fingertip. There are also assorted supermarkets, drugstores, gift-shops and boutiques
within very close range from this apartment.

We provide a range of services to ensure that our guests are truly comfortable and delighted, making it possible for
our guests to request car-hire, tours and chef services, as well as beverages and other required groceries with only
one click. However, transportation to anywhere in town is accessible from the immediate exit of this apartment.
